How We Treat

Treatments provided.

Each treatment plan is designed around your specific condition, progress, and recovery goals. Dr. Harika combines multiple approaches for the best outcomes.

Exercise Therapy

Guided therapeutic exercises to restore strength, flexibility, and range of motion.

Stretching Programs

Targeted stretching routines to relieve tightness and improve joint mobility.

Strengthening Programs

Progressive strengthening to rebuild muscle support around injured or weakened areas.

Rehabilitation Therapy

Comprehensive rehab plans for post-surgical, neurological, and chronic conditions.

Electrotherapy

Therapeutic electrical stimulation to manage pain and promote tissue healing.

IFT Therapy

Interferential therapy for deep pain relief and improved blood circulation.

TENS Therapy

Transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation for safe, non-invasive pain management.

Ultrasound Therapy

Deep tissue heating to reduce inflammation, ease stiffness, and speed recovery.

Posture Correction

Assessment and training to correct postural imbalances causing pain or discomfort.

Home Exercise Programs

Personalised exercise plans you can follow at home to maintain progress between visits.
